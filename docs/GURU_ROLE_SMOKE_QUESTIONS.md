# GURU smoke questions (one per simulated role)

Use these in **🧠 GURU Assistant** after picking the role and (where needed) **Active Department**.  
Wording uses English terms that appear in mock vault **titles / tags / summaries** so deterministic search can match.

| Role | Active Department (if applicable) | Question | Anchors concepts (in vault) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| CEO (Chief Executive Officer) | Any (full org) | What loan approval thresholds or secondary sign-off rules apply to high-value requests? | General — loan, approval, policy |
| CFO (Chief Financial Officer) | (multi-silo; pick Credit or use default) | What collateral valuation or lending policy monitoring actions are documented? | Credit & Loans — collateral, lending |
| CTO (Chief Technology Officer) | (multi-silo; pick IT or use default) | What firewall or database monitoring measures are described for IT systems? | IT & Digital — firewall, database, security |
| Department Head (VP) | **Operations** | How are branch operations efficiency or cash flow optimization addressed? | Operations — cash, efficiency, operations |
| Operational Staff | **HR & Admin** | What employee welfare or benefits policy areas are covered in HR documents? | HR & Admin — welfare, benefits, employee |

**Note:** Subset roles (**Department Head**, **Operational Staff**) must select the department column so the silo matches the question.

After bulk imports, run **System Config → Rebuild vault index & search cache** so `_SEARCH_CACHE.json` stays aligned with disk.
